About This

I created this because of the absolute lack of (free) magnetic poetry makers online.

Also, as someone who eternally struggles with writer block, arranging a bunch of 'magnets' feels a lot less like 'writing' and my brain likes that.

All of the preloaded words are some of my favorites, but go ahead and add your own!

Made by sadgrl.online. Find or clone this project on Github.

Report bugs or give feedback at sadgrl@riseup.net!

How to Use

Drag these words onto the canvas. You can double-click (desktop) or long-press (mobile) to delete a word from the canvas.
